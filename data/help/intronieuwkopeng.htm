Structure of the tax return

You answer the questions per topic. For example about 'Income' or 'Expenses'. Once you have completed the tax return, you can see the amount of tax you need to pay or will be refunded to you.

Business in the Netherlands

You can use this tax return form as a private individual, but also if you have a business in the Netherlands. And do you have a tax partner? In that case, you can file a tax return together, even if you both have a business in the Netherlands, or if 1 of you has a business in the Netherlands.
